For input.txt file:
1. string with text
2. block length (optimal - 64)
3. language of text ('rus' or 'eng')

You do not need to put numbers before the line!!!

Example:
Привет, как дела? Что делаешь?
32
rus

OR

Example:
Hello! How are you?
64
eng

The example with the text will be in the file 'input.txt'
